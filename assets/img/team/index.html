Who are we ?

ADA PACKAGING ?

The ADA PACKAGING group is fully integrated and specializes in textile and non-woven products for packaging. Its ambitious expansion policy, in direct contact with the realities of the market, has enabled ADA PACKAGING to adapt perfectly to the latest technological innovations, in order to ensure modern and efficient production, which has made its reputation among its partners...

It is also a group on a human scale where the notion of quality is essential, which is why ADA guarantees you a follow-up in the quality of its materials. With the mastery of nonwoven, a material made of 100% polypropylene, recyclable in the production of all kinds of personalized packaging. ADA offers a wide range of reusable non-woven products such as: - Special hotel bag - Special bag for bakery, pastry, catering - Shop bag - Commercial operations bag These products can also be disposable such as: - Products for hairdressing salons, beauty salons (bathrobe, thong, bathing cap, etc.) - Products for catering (knock, apron) Learn more ...
